Euchre

Classic Card Game

Quick Rules

• 4 players — You & Partner vs. Left & Right
• 24 cards (9 through Ace, all 4 suits)
• Right Bower: Jack of trump — highest card
• Left Bower: Jack of same color — second highest
• Round 1 bid: order up the turned card's suit, or pass
• Round 2 bid: name any other suit as trump, or pass
• Makers must win 3+ tricks · March (5 tricks) = 2 pts
• Euchred (makers fail) = 2 pts for defenders
• First to 10 points wins
