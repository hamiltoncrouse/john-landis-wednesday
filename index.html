The Rules

• You & Partner vs. 2 Opponents
• Opponents win a hand → she loses a layer
• You win a hand → she covers back up
• 5 layers of clothing → down to bikini only

Euchre basics:
• 24 cards (9–Ace), 5 tricks per hand
• Trump Jack = Right Bower (highest card)
• Same-color Jack = Left Bower (2nd highest)
• Make 3+ tricks as maker to score
• Get euchred = opponents score 2 pts
• First team to 10 points wins!
