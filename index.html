The Rules

• You & Partner vs. Amber & Sofia
• You win a hand → Amber & Sofia lose a layer
• They win a hand → Lola loses a layer
• 7 layers each → all the way down!

Euchre basics:
• 24 cards (9–Ace), 5 tricks per hand
• Trump Jack = Right Bower (highest)
• Same-color Jack = Left Bower (2nd)
• Make 3+ tricks as maker to score
• Get euchred = opponents score 2 pts
• First to 10 points wins!
